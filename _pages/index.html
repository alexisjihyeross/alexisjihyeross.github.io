I am currently a first-year PhD student at MIT CSAIL advised by Jacob Andreas and affiliated with the Language & Intelligence lab. Before starting at MIT, I was a predoctoral researcher at the Allen Institute for AI (AI2). I graduated from Harvard University in 2020 with a joint B.A. in Computer Science and Philosophy.
